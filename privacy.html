Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • Profile name and bio

Activity Information

  • Missions completed and points earned
  • Videos and images you upload
  • Comments you post, posts you like and save
  • Users you follow and direct messages you send
  • Watch time on videos

Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address and browser type
  • Crash reports, error logs and usage analytics

Location Information

  • Approximate region (from browser locale) — used only for the country leaderboard
  • Never shared with other users as a precise location

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and maintain the FlashArena service
  • Process your account registration and authentication
  • Verify that you are not a bot (email verification)
  • Store and display your uploaded content
  • Calculate your points, rank and rewards
  • Show you personalized feed recommendations
  • Detect and prevent abuse (spam, bots, fraud)
  • Respond to your support requests and send important service notifications
  • Improve our service, fix bugs and comply with legal obligations

3. Who We Share Your Information With

We DO NOT share:

  • Your email or password (except with Firebase Auth)
  • Your personal data with advertisers or third parties for marketing
  • Your location with other users

We DO share:

  • Public profile info (username, stats, bio) with other users
  • Your content (posts, comments) publicly on the platform
  • Data with Firebase (Google) for hosting, database and storage
  • Data with Sentry for error tracking
  • Data with law enforcement if legally required

4. Data Security

  • Firebase Authentication (industry standard)
  • HTTPS encryption on all traffic
  • Strict database security rules — clients cannot access other users' private data
  • Server-side validation and rate limiting on sensitive operations

5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Delete — permanently delete your account and all data (Profile → Delete My Account)
  • Rectify — update your profile information anytime

We respond to data requests within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: kept until you delete your account
  • Posts and comments: kept up to 90 days unless deleted earlier
  • Analytics and backups: kept up to 30 days

7. Children's Privacy

FlashArena is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover we have, we will delete it immediately. Parents/guardians: if your child has created an account, contact us to request deletion.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally and will notify you via in-app notification and an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of FlashArena after updates means you accept the new policy.
