# Privacy Policy - Flash Arena

**Last Updated: December 20, 2024**

## **1. Information We Collect**

We collect the following information to provide and improve our service:

### **Account Information**
- Email address
- Username
- Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
- Profile name
- Avatar

### **Activity Information**
- Missions completed
- Points earned
- Videos and images you upload
- Comments you post
- Posts you like and save
- Users you follow
- Watch time on videos

### **Technical Information**
- Device type and operating system
- IP address
- Browser type
- Crash reports and error logs
- Usage analytics (which features you use, how often)

### **Location Information**
- Approximate location (from IP address, optional)
- Only used for regional features and analytics
- Never shared with other users

## **2. How We Use Your Information**

We use your information to:

- ✅ Operate and maintain the Flash Arena service
- ✅ Process your account registration and authentication
- ✅ Verify that you are not a bot (email verification)
- ✅ Store and display your uploaded content
- ✅ Calculate your points and rank
- ✅ Show you personalized feed recommendations
- ✅ Detect and prevent abuse (spam, bots, fraud)
- ✅ Respond to your support requests
- ✅ Send you important service notifications
- ✅ Improve our service and fix bugs
- ✅ Comply with legal obligations

## **3. Who We Share Your Information With**

### **We DO NOT Share:**
- ❌ Your email or password (except with Firebase Auth)
- ❌ Your personal data with advertisers
- ❌ Your personal data with third parties for marketing
- ❌ Your location with other users

### **We DO Share:**
- ✅ Public profile info (username, avatar, stats) with other users
- ✅ Your content (posts, comments) publicly on the platform
- ✅ Aggregate analytics (no personal data) with our team
- ✅ Data with Firebase (Google) for hosting and database
- ✅ Data with Sentry (if enabled) for error tracking
- ✅ Data with law enforcement if legally required

## **4. Data Security**

We protect your data with:

- ✅ Firebase Authentication (industry standard)
- ✅ HTTPS encryption (all traffic is encrypted)
- ✅ Database rules (no client can access others' data)
- ✅ Secure password hashing
- ✅ Automatic backups
- ✅ No hardcoded secrets in code
- ✅ Server-side validation (not client-side only)
- ✅ Rate limiting on sensitive operations

## **5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)**

You have the right to:

- ✅ **Access**: Download all your data in JSON format (Profile → Data & Privacy → Export My Data)
- ✅ **Delete**: Permanently delete your account and all data (Profile → Data & Privacy → Delete My Account)
- ✅ **Rectify**: Update your profile information anytime
- ✅ **Opt-out**: Decline analytics tracking (privacy consent)

To exercise these rights:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Profile → Data & Privacy
3. Click Export or Delete as needed

We will respond within 30 days.

## **6. Data Retention**

- **Account data**: Kept until you delete your account
- **Posts**: Kept for 90 days, then deleted unless archived
- **Comments**: Kept for 90 days, then deleted
- **Analytics logs**: Kept for 30 days
- **Backups**: Kept for 30 days

## **7. Children's Privacy**

Flash Arena is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover we have, we will delete it immediately.

Parents/guardians: If your child has created an account, contact us immediately to request deletion.

## **8. Updates to This Policy**

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes via:
- In-app notification
- Email to your registered address
- Updated "Last Updated" date

Continued use of Flash Arena after updates means you accept the new policy.

## **9. Contact Us**

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

**Email**: privacy@flasharena.com (you can make this up for now)
**In-App**: Profile → Contact Support

We aim to respond within 7 business days.
